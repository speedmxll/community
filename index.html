The Dog House

Join us in creating the ultimate dog-themed board game!

"The Dog House" is an exciting, dog-themed economy board game inspired by Monopoly but with a unique twist. Players, referred to as "hoomans," compete to become the Top Dog in an economy orchestrated by their pet dogs, who are the alphas of the household. Infused with dog memes and humor, this game offers a playful and engaging experience for all ages.

Game Features

  • Unique dog-themed gameplay mechanics
  • Collect "cheeses" and become the Top Dog
  • Strategize with action cards like "Walk the Dog" and "Cheese Tax"
  • Engaging for both children and adults
  • Whimsical and colorful illustrations with popular dog memes
